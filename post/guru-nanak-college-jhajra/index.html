1. Doon University, Dehradun

Location: Kedarpur, Dehradun

Accreditation: State Public University, UGC Recognized

Eligibility: 10+2 with at least 50% marks

Entrance Test: Doon University Entrance Test (DUET)

Annual Fees: ₹40,000 – ₹50,000

Placement Highlights:

• Average Package: ₹3.5 LPA

• Sectors: NGOs, public administration, research & education

Why Choose:

Doon University offers a research-oriented, government-backed BBA program with affordable fees. It is suitable for students who prefer an academic and public serviceoriented career path.

2. Guru Nanak College, Dehradun

Location: Race Course Road, Dehradun (Central location)

Affiliation: Sri Dev Suman Uttarakhand University (SDSU)

Eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 45% marks (any stream)

Entrance Test: No entrance test required; admission based on merit

Annual Fees: ₹32,000 Placement Highlights:

• Highest Package: ₹4.2 LPA

• Average Package: ₹2.5 LPA

• Top recruiters: Local businesses, edtech startups, marketing firms

Why Choose:

Guru Nanak College is one of the fastest-growing BBA colleges in Dehradun. With a curriculum focused on practical learning, entrepreneurial skills, and industry exposure, it combines affordability with quality education. The college’s central location and studentcentric approach make it a preferred choice for budget-conscious students seeking realworld success. Apply or learn more: https://gnc.edu.in/Best-BBA-College-in-Dehradun-Uttarakahnd

3. Graphic Era University

Location: Clement Town, Dehradun

Accreditation: NAAC A+, UGC-recognized, AICTE approved

Eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 50% marks

Entrance Test: GEJET or merit-based

Annual Fees: ₹1.42 Lakhs

Placement Highlights:

• Average Package: ₹4.5 LPA

• Recruiters: Wipro, Infosys, TCS, Deloitte

Why Choose:

Graphic Era is a top private university in Dehradun known for its strong industry tie-ups and global collaborations. The BBA program offers specialization options and a modern teaching environment.

4. DIT University

Location: Mussoorie Diversion Road, Dehradun

Accreditation: UGC Approved, NAAC Accredited

Eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 50% marks

Entrance Test: Merit-based followed by a personal interview

Annual Fees: ₹1.10 Lakhs

Placement Highlights:

• Average Package: ₹3.8 LPA

• Recruiters: HDFC Bank, Tommy Hilfiger, Byju’s

Why Choose: DIT University blends business education with emerging technologies. Its BBA program encourages innovation, digital fluency, and leadership through case studies and industry internships.

5. UPES Dehradun

Location: Bidholi & Kandoli Campuses, Dehradun

Accreditation: NAAC A, UGC recognized, AIU member

Eligibility: 10+2 with at least 50% marks

Entrance Test: CUET / UPESEAT / SAT / Board Merit

Annual Fees: ₹2.52 Lakhs

Placement Highlights:

• Highest Package: ₹7.5 LPA

• Average Package: ₹4.2 LPA

• Recruiters: Accenture, EY, Amazon, KPMG

Why Choose:

UPES offers domain-specific BBA programs in logistics, digital business, international business, and more. With strong infrastructure and global links, it is a good fit for students aiming for international careers.
